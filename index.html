🌿 Why Trees Matter

Carbon Absorption: Trees absorb up to 48 pounds of CO2 each year.

Air Purification: They filter the air, providing oxygen and cleaning pollutants.

Soil Protection: Roots prevent soil erosion and help retain water.

Small steps make big differences. Here's what you can start doing today:

  • 🌿 Plant a tree on every birthday or special occasion.
  • 🪴 Support local nurseries and green NGOs.
  • ♻️ Reduce paper waste and recycle whenever possible.
  • 📚 Educate others about the importance of trees and biodiversity.
